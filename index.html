Your natal chart is a map of the sky at the exact moment you were born. It captures the positions of every planet, the angles between them, and the houses they occupy — all of which shape who you are and what you're here to experience.

A Thrax natal reading goes far beyond Sun sign astrology. You'll receive a full breakdown of your Sun, Moon, Rising, and every planetary placement along with the aspects between them. This is predictive astrology — the kind that identifies actual events in your life using harmonic and traditional aspects.

Most astrologers stop at the traditional aspect scheme of sextiles, squares, and trines. Thrax goes deeper, analyzing the octile harmonics and an extended range of aspect families:

Quintiles, Septiles, Octiles, Noviles, Deciles, Quincunx, Undeciles — and more. These harmonic aspects reveal layers of talent, fate, timing, and transformation that traditional readings miss entirely.

This reading covers your personality, strengths, challenges, career patterns, relationship tendencies, and timing cycles. Every aspect is listed and explained so you walk away understanding your chart at a level most people never reach.

Using both traditional aspects and the full harmonic spectrum, Thrax identifies dynamics that standard compatibility readings miss — the subtle threads of fate, karmic patterns, and transformative potential between two people.

Horary Astrology

$100

Horary astrology is the art of answering a specific question using a chart cast for the exact moment the question is asked. No birth data needed — the chart of the question itself holds the answer.

This is astrology at its most precise. Will I get the job? Should I move? Is this relationship going somewhere? The horary chart gives a direct, clear answer based on the planetary positions at the moment of inquiry.

Thrax applies the full harmonic aspect system to horary charts, pulling out details and timing information that traditional horary practitioners often overlook.

Delivered as a written report with a full breakdown of the chart, the aspects involved, and the answer to your question with timing indicators where applicable.
